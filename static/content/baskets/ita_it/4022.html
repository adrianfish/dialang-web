In South Africa, spring begins in late August and early September. This is a country whose wild flowers are too beautiful to describe. Travelling from near the border with Namibia down towards Cape Town, we make frequent overnight stops, thus keeping journey times short and maximising the time available for wildflower searches and walks. When combined with brilliant sunshine, magnificent scenery and flowers in great numbers and variety, this is a dream tour in a world of flowers.
