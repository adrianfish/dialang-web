The following suggestions may help you make progress towards C2:
  • Actively enlarge your vocabulary by, for example, deciding for a period to look up all new unknown words in a dictionary or by questioning particular meanings of known words in context or by attempting to notice and "collect" idiomatic usages and the contexts in which they appear.
  • Try to learn the fine distinctions of meaning between similar expressions (see for example dictionaries and guidebooks on style and writing).
  • Pay attention to how the writers put the intended message across (openly or in hidden ways), what resources are used and whether they consider themselves as outsiders or as co-participants in the actions they describe; whether they consider themselves part of the audience they write to or not.
  • Access the Portuguese version of the instructions for new machines rather than those in your first language.
  • Keep reading Portuguese popular novels as a leisure activity and read non-literary texts in your field or area of interest in preference to those in your first language.
