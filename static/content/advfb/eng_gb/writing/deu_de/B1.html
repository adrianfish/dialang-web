The following suggestions may help you make progress towards B2:
  • Actively seek opportunities to expand your vocabulary and your knowledge of idioms.
  • Actively try to find out what purposes texts are written for and what features characterise different kinds of texts.
  • Use what you read as a source for improving your writing; the more you read and pay attention to how others express themselves in writing the better your writing will get.
  • Study guidebooks on writing.
