The following suggestions may help you make progress towards A2:
  • Attend language classes so that you learn more sentence level grammar and Norwegian vocabulary, paying attention to sentence formation and connecting main and subordinate clauses.
  • Learn the basic letter writing conventions in Norwegian, for example, how to start and end a letter to a friend vs. somebody who you don't know.
  • Use whatever opportunities you can to read or listen to Norwegian or use Norwegian: at this level, any exposure to Norwegian is likely to help you improve your writing ability.
  • Keep a book where you write new words and structures in order to learn them, and also to improve your spelling.
  • Try to learn how new words are typically formed in Norwegian.
