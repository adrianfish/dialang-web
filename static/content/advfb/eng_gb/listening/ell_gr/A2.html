The following suggestions may help you make progress towards B1:
  • Do not expect to understand things at the first go; listen again if you can and see if you can understand more then.
  • Prepare yourself by anticipating the type of speech you will be listening to (use context, previous knowledge about the topic and the people, which you have heard or read in your first language).
  • If there is something on the radio or on TV in Greek, make a point of listening to the speech and try to understand the main point and do not worry about individual words that you miss.
  • Listen to songs in the language you are learning and follow the written lyrics.
