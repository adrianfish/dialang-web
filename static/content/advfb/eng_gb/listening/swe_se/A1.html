The following suggestions may help you make progress towards A2:
  • Attend language classes so that you can benefit from the help of a teacher to improve your listening strategies, or join a distance language learning programme that can help you systematise your listening practice.
  • AdvisoryTips_Bullet_#Listening_#A1_#Item2_#SE
  • Use visual cues, images, intonation or sound / music to help your understanding of what the speaker means and of the speaker's attitude.
  • In conversations or when addressed, do not be afraid of asking for repetitions and reformulations; native speakers do it as well! Avoid the 'Jag förstår inte' and go for the 'Skulle du kunna vara snäll och säga om det du sa?', 'Säg om det där, är du snäll!', and even 'Skulle du inte kunna vara snäll och tala lite långsammare?'. The more opportunities you have to listen to something the better you will understand it.
