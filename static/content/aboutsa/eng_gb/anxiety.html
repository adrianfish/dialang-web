Anxiety

Certain tests can be so important for your later life (work, studies) that you feel very nervous when taking them. Although DIALANG is not such a high-stakes test, you may still feel nervous when doing the tasks. The stress that a test causes may hamper some people's performance, but others may do better just because of the challenge. Some people may not be affected at all.
